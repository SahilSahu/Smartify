Street Quality Identification  (S.Q.U.I.D)


By BitCoders on August 9, 2018

What is SQUID?


Street QUality IDentification is a go-to built keeping in mind the issues 53,720 vehicles face who hit the streets every single day, as indicated by the statistics in an article by The Times Of India.

SQUID enables a comprehensive map of a city’s street surface quality and portrays a bird’s eye view of the streets, enabling the admin to monitor the conditions in real time(pothole complications) and take informed decisions for the development and improvement of the street quality.

Other beneficiaries would be ambulances, fire brigade officials, as they have to deal with cases that are time-critical, transport companies for moving fragile instruments such as medical equipments, as they can make proper decisions and decide an appropriate route....
